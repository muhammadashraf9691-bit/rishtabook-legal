## 1. Information We Collect

When you create and use a RishtaBook account we may collect the following information:

**Identity & contact data**
- Full name (first and last)
- Email address
- Mobile number (verified via SMS one-time code)
- Optional profile photograph

**Profile data (provided by you in 4 steps)**
- City, province, country
- Gender, age, height, weight, body shape, skin colour
- Religion, caste, nationality, occupation, education
- Marital status and family details
- Monthly income (optional)
- Preferences for an ideal match (the parameters you'd like the other person to have)

**Technical data**
- A randomly generated device identifier (used to manage sessions and prevent abuse)
- Application logs and crash reports (used to fix bugs)

We do **not** collect precise GPS location, contacts, calendar, microphone, camera (other than for the photo you choose to upload), SMS content, or any biometric data.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data described above strictly for:

- Creating and managing your RishtaBook account
- Verifying your identity through SMS one-time codes
- Suggesting compatible matches based on your preferences
- Allowing you to discover and interact with other verified profiles
- Providing customer support
- Improving the App's reliability, security and user experience
- Sending you essential service-related notifications

We do **not** use your data for behavioural advertising, profiling for advertising purposes, or any automated decision-making with legal effects.

---

## 3. Communication & Marketing

We may occasionally send you product updates, new features or important account notices through in-app messaging or email. You can opt out of non-essential communication at any time from your profile settings or by contacting our support team.

---

## 4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures provided by Google Firebase (Authentication and Cloud Firestore security rules) to protect your data in transit and at rest. While no system can be 100% secure, we continuously work to safeguard your information against unauthorized access.

To further protect your information inside the App we have enabled `FLAG_SECURE` on every screen, which prevents screenshots and screen recording.

---

## 5. Sharing of Information

Your profile is visible **only to other verified RishtaBook users** — never to the public internet or search engines. We never sell your personal information to advertisers or third parties.

We may share strictly necessary data with the following categories of service providers, only for operating the App:

- **Google Firebase** (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Storage) — hosting and authentication
- **SMS gateways** integrated with Firebase Authentication — for sending OTP codes

These providers are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential and use it only for the purposes we specify.

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your profile data for as long as your account remains active. You can request deletion of your account at any time by contacting [fs4281996@gmail.com](mailto:fs4281996@gmail.com). Upon deletion:

- Your profile document is permanently removed from our database.
- Your email and phone are added to an internal blacklist so the same identity cannot be re-registered.
- Backups containing your data are purged according to our standard backup rotation (no later than 30 days).
