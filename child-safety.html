Child Safety Standards

Rishta Book - Find Partner has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

1. Age Requirement

Our app is for users aged 18 years and above only. We do not allow anyone under 18 to create an account.

2. Prohibited Content

Any content, profile, photo, or chat that sexualizes children or promotes CSAE is strictly prohibited and will be removed immediately.

3. Reporting

Users can report abuse in-app via the "Report" button on every profile/chat, or by emailing us at muhammadashraf9691@gmail.com. We review reports within 24 hours.

4. Action & Compliance

We remove offending accounts, preserve evidence, and report to relevant authorities including Pakistan's FIA Cyber Crime and NCMEC where required. We comply with all applicable child safety laws.
